Preparation

  1. Separate the egg whites from the yolks.
  2. Beat the yolks with the sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  3. Add the mascarpone and mix well.
  4. Beat the egg whites until stiff and gently fold them into the mascarpone mixture.
  5. Quickly dip the sponge fingers in the coffee and Marsala and place them in a dish.
  6. Spread a layer of the mascarpone mixture over the sponge fingers.
  7. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used up, finishing with a layer of mascarpone.
  8. Chill for at least 2 hours.
  9. Before serving, dust with cocoa powder.
